Bike Station Activity

The following map visualizes the average daily activity of bike-sharing stations in Helsinki and Espoo during the active cycling season in 2023. Each station is represented as a proportional bubble placed according to its geographic location.

The size and color intensity of the bubbles correspond to the average daily number of trips associated with each station. This makes it possible to identify the most active parts of the network and compare station usage across different areas of the city.
