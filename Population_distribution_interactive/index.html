Helsinki Population Distribution

This interactive visualization presents the hourly distribution of population across Helsinki using a 250 metre statistical grid and dynamic population data derived from mobile network activity. The color intensity of each cell represents the relative concentration of people at a selected hour, while the slider and day-type selector allow the map to transition through different moments of the day. Moving through the hours creates an almost animated view of the city, making it possible to observe how the spatial rhythm of Helsinki changes between daytime activity, evening concentration and quieter nighttime periods.
