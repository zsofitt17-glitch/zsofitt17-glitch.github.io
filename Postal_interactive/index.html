Postal Code Area Comparison

The following visualization presents the characteristics of Helsinki postal-code areas through an interactive pictogram-based interface. Each postal-code area can be selected directly from the map, after which the corresponding indicators are displayed in a separate information panel.

The visualization combines median household income, dwellings, workplaces, and jobs in the education sector into a compact comparative representation. The coloured symbols indicate the relative ranking of the selected postal-code area compared with the other areas of Helsinki.
