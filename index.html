Geospatial Data Visualization Thesis Portfolio

A collection of interactive, static and animated visualizations developed for my thesis, telling the story of Helsinki through urban structure, daily dynamics, and additional accessibility and environmental perspectives.

Author: Zsófia Tasnádi-Tulogdi

Supervisor: Gergő Pintér

Institution: Corvinus University of Budapest

Thesis year: 2026

About the Thesis

This website presents the visual outputs of my thesis project. The aim is to communicate Helsinki through data, not only as a set of maps or numbers, but as a city with structure, movement, and rhythm.
