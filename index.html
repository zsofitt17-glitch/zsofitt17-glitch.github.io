A collection of interactive and static visualizations developed for my thesis, focusing on spatial patterns, population distribution, bike-related data, and postal-code-based analysis.

About the Thesis

This website presents the visual outputs of my thesis project. The aim is to communicate geospatial patterns through a combination of interactive web maps, static figures, and data-driven visual summaries.
